Notes for Obtaining CDMP Certification

Published on 2021/10/14 📖 1 min read

Motivation

When building data platforms, data lakes, and data analytics infrastructure, it’s not just about the infrastructure itself. You also need a data management perspective — how to collect, manage, improve the quality of, and leverage data for business purposes. I wanted to move beyond fragmented knowledge and gain a comprehensive understanding. In terms of DMBOK (described below), data engineering is just one area within the broader data management domain, so building solutions with only that perspective in mind is not truly effective.

What is CDMP?

CDMP (Certified Data Management Professionals) is an international certification for data management accredited by DAMA (Data Management Association). DAMA has compiled the Data Management Body of Knowledge (DMBOK), a comprehensive knowledge framework for data management. You can think of it as the PMBOK equivalent for data management.

Requirements for CDMP Certification

There are multiple levels, and you can achieve each level by passing the Data Management Fundamentals Exam and Specialist Exams. The first step is to pass the Data Management Fundamentals Exam with a score of 60% or higher.

https://cdmp.info/about/
