Business outsourcing contracts in Japan come in two types: "Contract for Work" (請負契約) and "Quasi-Mandate Contract" (準委任契約). Notes on these two types of contracts.

![image-20210113152600538](image-20210113152600538.png)

|                         | Contract for Work (請負契約)              | Quasi-Mandate Contract (準委任契約)         |
| ----------------------- | ----------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| Contractor's obligation | Obligation to complete the work           | Duty of care of a good manager              |
| Deliverable             | Required                                  | Not required                                |
| Payment timing          | Simultaneously with delivery of deliverable | After completion of the service act        |
| Warranty liability      | Yes                                       | Not stipulated                              |
| Mid-term cancellation   | Client can cancel the contract            | Either party can cancel at any time         |
| Subcontracting          | Allowed                                   | Generally not allowed                       |
